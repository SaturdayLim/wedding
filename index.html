Health Score — How It's Calculated
Five components × 20 pts = 100. On-premise accounts only. Gating and smoothing ensure new accounts can't inflate their score before earning it.
20 pts
Recency
Gated — 0 until 2nd active month.

20 = this month
16 = <60 d · 12 = <90 d
6 = <120 d · 2 = <180 d
0 = 180+ days
20 pts
Cadence
Smoothed — 3 prior months added so 1st order scores ~5 pts, not 20.

Active ÷ (Tenure + 3) × 20
e.g. 8 of 10 mo → 8÷13 = 12 pts
20 pts
Streak
Consecutive months ordering, from most recent backward. Full 20 pts at 12 months.

6 mo = 10 pts · 3 mo = 5 pts · 1 mo = 2 pts
20 pts
Momentum
Gated — requires 2+ active months. No prior period = no trend.

Last 3M vs prior 3M revenue. Growth = higher pts, decline = lower.

Metric Definitions
Order Cadence
How reliably an account orders each month, regardless of order size. Only shown for accounts with 3+ active months so a single sell-in doesn't inflate the score.
Cadence = Active Months ÷ Tenure Months × 100%
e.g. 8 orders across 12 months = 67% cadence
A high-cadence account is a reliable reorder partner. A low-cadence account with high basket size may be a special-occasion buyer.
Basket Size (with Confidence)
Average monthly spend per active month. For accounts with few data points, raw averages are unreliable — a single large order skews everything. We apply Laplace smoothing (Bayesian prior) to pull thin estimates toward the portfolio average.
Smoothed Basket = (k × μ₀ + Σ revenue) ÷ (k + n)
where μ₀ = portfolio avg basket, k = 3 (prior weight), n = active months
Confidence indicator reflects how many data months we have:
●●● High = 6+ months · ●●○ Medium = 3–5 months · ●○○ Low = 1–2 months
Momentum
Compares revenue in the most recent 3 calendar months against the prior 3 months. Captures trajectory rather than absolute size — a small account growing fast scores better than a large account declining.
Momentum % = (Last 3M Revenue − Prior 3M Revenue) ÷ Prior 3M Revenue × 100
New accounts with no prior 3M revenue are shown separately as "NEW" risers.
Tenure & Streak
Tenure = months between first order and today (total relationship length).
Active Months = distinct calendar months with at least one order.
Streak = the longest run of consecutive months with orders, measured from the most recent month backward. A 🔥 badge appears for streaks of 3+ months.
